#Vim Key Mapping

* `<c-f>` Search in files (using ack)
* `<leader>w` Save
* `:w!!` Save with sudo
* `<c-c>` Comment out/in
* `<leader>y` Copy to clipboard
* `<leader>y` Cut to clipboard
* `<leader>p` Paste from clipboard
* `<s-tab>` Indent

## Tabs
* `<s-l>` Switch right tab
* `<s-h>` Switch left tab
* `<s-n>` New tab
* `<f2>` Show/Hide NerdTree

## Multiple Cursors
* `<c-n>`
* `<c-p>`
* `<c-x>`

## Tmux
* `<c-l>` Switch right pane
* `<c-h>` Switch left pane
